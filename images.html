Images Bookmarklet for Accessibility Testing inserts a green dashed outline around images that have alt values and a red solid outline around images with no alt attribute. Alt attribute vaules are displayed in black on yellow text on top of the images. Images with empty alt values, <img alt=""> are shown as alt="".
